The agreed interest rate for each Facility is based on SME Prime Rate and is subject to change from time to time.

The Default Rate is (i) a rate of 5% per annum above the agreed interest rate for the Relevant Facility (ies) in default, where an interest rate is specified herein, and/or (ii) where no interest rate is specified, the bank may select at its own discretion, a rate of 5% per annum above the agreed interest rate for any other facilities availed by the customer where an interest rate is specified or apply a default rate of 5% per annum above SME Prime Rate. Any overdrawn amounts in your current account(s) or overdraft account(s) would be subject to a default rate of 18% per annum.

The agreed interest rate for the Facility is based on 3 months EIBOR and is subject to change from time to time.

EIBOR is on 3 months basis as applicable. The first applicable daily 3 months EIBOR rate will be taken two business days prior to the Utilisation of any of the Facilities and will apply for the next 3 months during which any Facilities are provided. Thereafter the EIBOR rates will be reset every subsequent 3 months or as the Bank may deem appropriate. The applicable daily 3 months EIBOR rate will be taken two business days prior to reset date.

The Late Payment Penalty Rate is (i) a rate of 5% per annum above the agreed interest rate for the relevant facility, where a interest rate is specified herein and (ii) a rate of 5% per annum above 3months EIBOR subject to change from time to time, in respect of Facilities in respect of which no interest rate is specified herein.

In line with the UAE's Value Added Tax (VAT) implementation from 1st January 2018, VAT at the rate of 5% will be levied on RAKBANK's fees and charges specified herein, wherever applicable and as per UAE law.

The Tariff of charges is stipulated on the Bank's website at www.rakbank.ae and is subject to change at the Bank's sole discretion.
